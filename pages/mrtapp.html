About project

The team has done a redesign for the "Taipei Metro GO" app, focusing on five major areas, which are: (1) Home page, (2) Crowdedness in train cars, (3) Station information, (4) Metro network, and (5) Other services.

The original home page is integrated with the functions of journey planning and arrival time. The purpose is to:

Make the layout design more intuitive, achieving maximum demand satisfaction with the fewest steps.

Integration of various functions can make it easier for passengers to use and increase the app download rate.

Project Goal

The metro is an essential daily companion for Taipei commuters, and the Taipei Metro transports more than two million passengers every day. It is believed that everyone has some experience riding the metro. When looking back at our past experiences, we might care about the train arrival time or which station we need to transfer to another line. For commuters who take the metro every day or for tourists visiting Taipei, the metro network is like the North Star that guides lost people in the right direction. Therefore, in order to improve the app usage rate and provide a better user experience, the team will redesign the original five main functional pages.
