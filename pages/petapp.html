About project

What is pet app? Pet app is a pet wellbeing where user not only can gain the correct pet’s knowlege but easily take care of their pet. We have tried to bring all the severices in one app to make the life of pet owners easier than before.

Why we selected this topic?

Pets have become a part of many families nowadays. Many of us like to have pet animals as a hobby. But in this case, the big challenge is to take proper knowlege of the pet .In this modern age, people want to get everything easily. And we noticed that people often have problems with their pet health and correct knowlege. So we have decided to design an app to solve these types of problems so that people can get everything easily for their pets, also can make diffierent kinds of pet owners.

Project goal

Through the common topic of pets, more people can get to know each other, share the life of pets, help each other and get the correct pet knowledge from Pet APP.

Design Process

User research

The goal of the research phase was to gather important insights from people interested in owning or caring for pets. We conducted a user survey using Google Forms and collected 106 responses. Additionally, we personally conducted some interviews to ask more specific questions.

Problem

  • Users are unable to obtain the most accurate knowledge regarding pets.
  • Pet articles or images on the internet are difficult to search for immediately using keywords.

Solution

  • Professional pet information will only be posted on the pet knowledge section after being approved by professional personnel/veterinarians.
  • Search filtering is implemented during the search function to enable users to find the desired information more accurately.

Expectations for APP

  • I want to learn more about pets, such as how to care for them if they have any illnesses, and be able to search for information instantly.
  • I hope there is a feature to categorize photos by time, to record the growth process of animals.
  • Classifying information according to the common needs of pet categories can make it easier to absorb.

Survey insights

  • Due to the large quantity and uncertain sources of pet-related information, the accuracy of pet-related knowledge needs to be more precise."
  • Users are very concerned about the search function.
  • Users ideally want to see multiple people explaining the same thing at the same time, which can provide more answers for the searcher to choose from."
